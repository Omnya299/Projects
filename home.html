HOW TO PREVENT CORONAVIRUS

Stay At Home

A stay-at-home order, safer-at-home order, a movement control order or lockdown regulations, also known by loosely using the terms quarantine, isolation, or lockdown is an order from a government ...All individuals living in the State of California are currently ordered to stay home or at their place of residence, except for permitted work, local shopping..

Wear Face Mask

mask helps contain small droplets that come out of your mouth and nose when you talk, sneeze or cough. If you have COVID-19 and are not showing symptoms, a face mask reduces your chance of spreading the infection to others. If you are healthy, a mask may protect you from larger droplets from people around you ,Wearing a Mask: Myths and Facts Illustration of the correct and incorrect ways to wear a mask It's no myth: It not only matters if you wear a mask but also how you wear it. Wearing a mask to protect against coronavirus.

Wash Your Hands

People use antibacterial products because of its ability to permanently wash away different types of bacteria from our hands and body. As soon as the bacteria come into contact with the antibacterial products, it is killed. Antibacterial products increase the effectiveness of hygienic measures and help to prevent diseases. This is done because they contain ingredients that prevent bacteria from reproducing on the surface.
